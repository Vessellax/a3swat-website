Leadership & Staff

Our Coaches

Every member of our staff brings elite playing and coaching experience dedicated to developing your athlete at every level.

CEO / Director of Marketing
Allik Anchondo

A University of Texas graduate and former Austin High School letterman, Allik played 2 years of college baseball at Coastal Bend College before channeling his competitive drive into building A3 SWAT into a premier regional brand in Texas baseball.

As co-founder and CEO, Allik is committed to building not just winning teams, but a program that produces disciplined athletes who represent A3 SWAT with pride — on the field and in the classroom.

UT Graduate Austin High Letterman Co-Founder
CEO / Director of Operations
Alexander Gonzales

A Texas State University graduate, Alexander serves as the operational backbone of A3 SWAT. His focus is on instilling character and discipline in every player in the program — values he believes are inseparable from athletic excellence.

Alexander oversees the day-to-day structure of A3 SWAT, ensuring that every practice, tournament, and development session runs at the standard the program demands. His approach is simple: hold everyone to the highest bar, every day.

Texas State Graduate Operations Director Co-Founder
Director of Personal Development
Dustin Jourdan

A former pitcher at Ohio State University, Dustin brings Division I experience and extensive coaching credentials to the A3 SWAT staff. His specialty is the intersection of physical performance and mental toughness — the elements that separate good athletes from great ones.

As Director of Personal Development, Dustin works with each player individually to build the focus, resilience, and leadership skills needed to succeed at the high school and collegiate levels. He doesn't just coach pitching — he builds leaders.
